## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Storyline compatibility issues:** Test early and often across versions
- **Performance problems:** Monitor file sizes and optimize assets
- **Browser compatibility:** Test on all target browsers regularly
- **SCORM integration failures:** Test LMS integration early in development

### Content Risks
- **Learning objective misalignment:** Regular review against objectives
- **Character inconsistency:** Maintain character reference guide
- **Legal accuracy concerns:** SME review of all legal content
- **Accessibility non-compliance:** Regular accessibility audits

### Timeline Risks
- **Asset creation delays:** Parallel development with placeholder assets
- **Complex interaction development:** Prototype early, iterate quickly
- **Testing bottlenecks:** Continuous testing throughout development
- **Stakeholder approval delays:** Regular check-ins and early previews
