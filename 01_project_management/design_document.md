# Legal Writing 101 Simulation - Design Document

## Project Overview
**Title:** "The Case for Better Learning: A JUS Simulation in Learning Design"
**Duration:** 10-minute interactive Storyline simulation
**Target Audience:** Learning designers and instructional design professionals

## Core Mission
Redesign "Legal Writing 101" — a traditional, lecture-heavy course — into a modern, blended learning experience that improves engagement, application, and measurable learning outcomes.

## Learning Objectives
By completing the simulation, learners will be able to:
1. Identify performance and learning gaps in the current Legal Writing 101 course
2. Select learning design frameworks and modalities that fit the Legal Writing 101 context
3. Prototype blended solutions (digital + live) tailored to diverse legal learners
4. Evaluate stakeholder feedback and revise the design to align with business and learner needs

## Technical Specifications
- **Platform:** Articulate Storyline 360
- **Duration:** 8-10 minutes
- **Interactions:** Drag-and-drop, branching scenarios, variable tracking
- **Output:** SCORM 1.2 package
- **Browser Support:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge

## Success Metrics
- Completion rate > 85%
- Learner satisfaction > 4.0/5.0
- Knowledge retention test > 80%

- Budget: Minimal (use free/stock assets)
